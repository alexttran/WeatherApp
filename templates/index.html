    About Breeze

    Breeze shows current weather and a 5-day forecast using Open-Meteo. You can search by city, ZIP/postal code, landmarks, or coordinates, use your current location, and save date-range requests to the database.

    • Search: type a place or paste lat,lon; use 📍 for device location.
    • Save to DB: pick a start/end date, then click “Save to DB”.
    • Saved Requests: view, edit, or delete past entries.

    Weather data courtesy of Open-Meteo; geocoding via Geocodify.
